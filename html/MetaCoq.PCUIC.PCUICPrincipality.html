Library MetaCoq.PCUIC.PCUICPrincipality



We show that principal types are derivable, without relying on normalization. The principal type is burried in the proof here, but PCUICSafeRetyping.type_of gives an explicit computation, but its definition and correctness proof requires completeness of weak-head-reduction.

  Theorem principal_type {Γ u A} : Σ ;;; Γ |- u : A →
    ∑ C, (∀ B, Σ ;;; Γ |- u : B → Σ ;;; Γ ⊢ C ≤ B × Σ ;;; Γ |- u : C).

A weaker version that is often convenient to use.
